BURN 2015

5K & HALF MARATHON

On Saturday, October 10, 2015, experience a festive walk or run through the beautiful Coyote Hills Regional Park. Both 5K and Half Marathon courses are supported by our dedicated BURN volunteers. You will never run out of hydration, snacks and cheers! Half marathon finishers will receive an inaugural BURN 5K & Half Marathon commemorative run shirt. Bring your family members, friends, co-workers and community groups to this non-competitive, high energy, fun run and walk.
Run with us, BURN bright, you are the best!

BURN is a fast growing non-profit running club rooted in the SF Bay Area promotes better health and better life.

Please note that due to our arrangement with the park district, parking is not permitted inside Coyote Hill Regional Park and the dirt lot on Paseo Padre Parkway on race day. Drop-offs can be made on Commerce Dr.

SHUTTLE

Shuttle service will be provided from 7:00 am at DeVry University parking lot every 15 minutes. Priority goes to Half Marathon runners from 7:00 to 7:30 am, and to 5K runners from 7:30 to 8:00 am. Last shuttle from Finish Line to DeVry University is 11:30am.
