BURN Running Club is a 501(c)3 not-for-profit organization. We promotes a healthy, motivated, socially responsible lifestyle. Our aspiration is to change lives around us.

Our mission: BURN creates a safe and encouraging environment to support and guide runners of different levels to achieve their own health and performance goals. BURN aim to influence the local community at large to adopt an active lifestyle through events and education.

Check out http://burnrunning.org for the group runs and more.
